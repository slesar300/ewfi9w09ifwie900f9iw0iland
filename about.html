About us

We are a studio for AI-native moving image.

Ctrl + Create sits at the intersection of craft, technology, and story. We design and produce intelligent video — from first frame to final delivery — in whatever format your audience actually watches.

What we do

Commercials, social suites, explainers, title sequences, showreels, and experimental pieces. Generative and hybrid pipelines, traditional edit and grade, sound design, and clear versioning so your team can ship with confidence.

Formats

Horizontal, vertical, square, ultra-wide — we build for cinema screens, feeds, OOH, and everything in between. One concept, many crops, one coherent look across the whole campaign.

How we work

We start from your brief and references, then propose a visual direction and motion language before pixels multiply. You get milestones you can react to — not a black box. We care about legibility, pacing, and brand safety as much as about novelty. When the cut is approved, we hand over masters and platform-specific exports with sensible naming and specs.

Who we work with

Agencies, in-house creative teams, founders, and producers who want a partner that speaks both “film” and “product”. If you have a story and a timeline, we can help you put intelligent video in front of the right people.
